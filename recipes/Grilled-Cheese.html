Best Grilled Cheese

This is the finest grilled cheese sandwich I've ever tasted, with a gooey Cheddar and bacon filling and a crunchy Parmesan crust! This, in terms of grilled cheese, is it! Grilled cheese sandwiches are incredibly simple to make. The complete recipe may be seen below, but here's a quick summary of what to expect from it: Mix the Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese and butter together. Place one piece of cheddar cheese and two slices of bacon on each sandwich. Apply the butter mixture evenly on every bread slice. Cook in a skillet until both sides are golden brown.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 8 slices cooked bacon
  • 4 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices sourdough bread

Directions

  1. Make sandwiches by placing two slices of bacon and one slice of Cheddar cheese on half of the bread slices. Top with the remaining slices of bread.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spread some of the butter mixture on the top of each sandwich. Place sandwiches butter side down in the skillet. Spread the remaining butter mixture on the other sides. Cook until golden brown on each side, about 3 minutes per side.
  3. To serve, cut in half diagonally.
